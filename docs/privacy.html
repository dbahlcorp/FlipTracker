Flip Tracker — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Flip Tracker is built to keep your data on your device. This policy explains what that means in practice.

Data collection

Flip Tracker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no accounts, no login, and no network connection of any kind. All information you enter — property details, purchase and sale prices, expenses, photos, and notes — is stored locally on your device using on-device storage (SQLite and device storage).

Photos

If you add photos to a flip, they are saved to the app's local storage on your device only. Flip Tracker does not upload photos anywhere.

Analytics and tracking

Flip Tracker does not use analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services. No usage data is collected or sent anywhere.

Data sharing and export

The app lets you export your own data using your device's built-in share sheet, entirely under your control. Flip Tracker itself never sends your data to any server.

Data deletion

Since all data lives only on your device, deleting the app deletes all associated data. There is nothing stored on any remote server to delete.

Children's privacy

Flip Tracker does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect information at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a new "last updated" date.
